What We Offer

At Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum – South Manchester, we provide Slovak language education, cultural events, and support for Slovak-speaking families.

  • Morning Classes: 10:00 – 12:00 at The Firs Primary School
  • Afternoon Classes: 14:00 – 16:00 at Sale High School
  • Slovak Language Lessons – Structured classes for children and adults.
  • Adapted Teaching Methods – Incorporating UK educational practices.
  • Cultural Events & Workshops – Celebrating Slovak traditions.
  • Community & Social Gatherings – Opportunities to meet and connect.
  • Opportunities for Adults – Workshops and networking events.

How We Help

  • Support for 'Komisionálne Skúšky' – Helping children maintain their Slovak academic level.
  • Guidance for Families Returning to Slovakia – School transitions and legal advice.
  • Bridging the UK & Slovak Education Systems – Resources for smoother transitions.
  • Legal & Administrative Advice – Assisting with UK legal processes.
  • English Language Support – Help with English language and translation services.

Our Approach & Teaching Methods

We integrate language learning with cultural education, providing an immersive experience.

  • Communicative Language Teaching (CLT): Building confidence through communication.
  • Task-Based Learning (TBL): Using real-life tasks to teach functional language.
  • Content and Language Integrated Learning (CLIL): Learning Slovak through meaningful cultural topics.
  • Cooperative Language Learning (CLL): Encouraging teamwork and collaboration.
