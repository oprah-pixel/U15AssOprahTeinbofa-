We are passionate about preserving the Slovak language and culture for future generations. As a registered charity, we rely on the generosity of our community to continue providing high-quality education, cultural programs, and support services.

Make a Donation

  • Develop educational resources for our students.
  • Offer support and guidance for Slovak families in the UK.

Every donation, no matter the amount, makes a real difference in ensuring that Slovak heritage thrives abroad.

Payment Details

To complete your child’s enrollment at Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum – South Manchester, payment must be made according to the selected payment plan (Term Fees or Pay-As-You-Go). Below are the details for making payments.

How to Pay

1. Bank Transfer (Preferred Method)

You can make payments directly to our account:

  • Account Name: Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum – South Manchester
  • Account Number: 19215268
  • Sort Code: 30-54-66
  • Reference: [Child’s Full Name]

Important: Please include your child’s full name as the payment reference to ensure we can allocate your payment correctly.

2. Cash Payments (By Prior Arrangement Only)
  • If you prefer to pay in cash, this must be arranged on the day with the member of the SVCSM Team.
  • Cash payments can only be made at our sessions and must be provided in an envelope with your child’s name.

Payment Deadlines

  • Term Fees: Payments must be made before the first session of each term to secure your child’s place.
  • Pay-As-You-Go: Payment must be made on the day of attendance before the lesson starts.

Sibling Discount

Each additional sibling receives a 25% discount on their fees.

Refund & Cancellation Policy

  • Missed Classes: Unfortunately, we are unable to offer refunds or credits for missed sessions.
  • Withdrawals: If you wish to withdraw your child, please inform us in writing at least one week before the new term starts.
  • Event-Based Refunds: If an event is cancelled by us, a refund or credit may be issued.
