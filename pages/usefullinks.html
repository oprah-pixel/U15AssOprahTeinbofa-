Library (Under Development)

We are working on establishing a Slovak book collection and learning resource hub for our students, members, and the wider community. Our goal is to make Slovak literature, language-learning materials, and cultural books more accessible while encouraging a love for reading in both children and adults.

Our Future Plans

We are in discussions with a local library about the possibility of creating a dedicated Slovak book section. This would allow our community to borrow Slovak books directly from a public library, helping to preserve and promote our language while making resources available to more people. The details of this collaboration are still being worked out, and we will share updates as soon as they become available.

How You Can Help

  • Donate Slovak Books – If you have Slovak children's books, novels, textbooks, or educational materials, we would love to add them to our collection.
  • Partner with Us – If you represent an organisation, business, or cultural institution interested in supporting our Slovak book section, let's collaborate!
  • Contribute Towards Library Costs – If you would like to help fund the creation of the library, donations are welcome. Contributions will go towards purchasing books, maintaining the collection, and covering logistical costs. (Navigate to our Payments Page for details.)

This is just the beginning of our library project! If you have ideas, resources, or want to get involved, we would love to hear from you.

For book donations, partnerships, or sponsorship inquiries, please Contact Us.

SVCSM Work Book – „Pracovný zošit“ (Seasonal Learning Resource)

We are proud to offer our very own workbook, designed by our educators to support Slovak language learning in an engaging and structured way. This interactive resource is tailored to different learning levels and includes:

  • Grammar & Vocabulary Exercises – Practical tasks aligned with our curriculum for all ages.
  • Cultural Activities – Activities exploring Slovak traditions, history, and customs.
  • Creative Writing & Reading Tasks – Encouraging students to use Slovak in real-life contexts.
  • Fun activities – To make learning enjoyable and engaging.

The workbook is available in two formats:

  • Free Online Version – Viewable on our platform for all students and members.
  • Downloadable & Printable Version – Available for purchase for those who prefer a printable copy.
