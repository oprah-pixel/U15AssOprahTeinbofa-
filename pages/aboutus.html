Who Are We

Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum – South Manchester is a registered charity dedicated to preserving and promoting Slovak language and culture. We provide Slovak language education and organize cultural and community activities to help Slovak-speaking families maintain their heritage while living abroad. While our primary focus is on supporting Slovak-speaking children and families, we warmly welcome anyone interested in learning the Slovak language and engaging with our traditions. Beyond our Saturday school, we offer various workshops, events, and social activities where individuals of all ages can connect, learn, and take part in our cultural community.

Our Mission

Our mission is to: At Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum – South Manchester, our mission goes beyond just teaching the Slovak language. We are here to keep our heritage alive, to ensure that children growing up abroad don’t lose their connection to their roots, and to create a place where Slovak culture thrives—even far from home. Education is at the heart of what we do, but our purpose extends even further. We are here to bridge the gap between the UK and Slovak education systems, ensuring that children who may one day return to Slovakia can transition smoothly. Above all, we are here to create a community—one that welcomes, supports, and celebrates Slovak language, traditions, and the people who cherish them.

Our Values

We believe in the power of language to bring people together and create a sense of belonging. Our values are based on respect for Slovak culture and the people who carry it with them. We are committed to fostering an environment where everyone can learn, grow, and celebrate their heritage with pride. Together, we build connections, friendships, and a sense of family.

Community Engagement

We offer various activities that engage individuals of all ages, including language lessons, cultural workshops, social events, and community gatherings. Through these activities, we aim to strengthen ties within the Slovak community while also inviting others to learn about and appreciate our culture.

Our Impact

Over the years, we’ve made a positive impact on many families by providing educational resources, cultural events, and a supportive community. We continue to grow and expand our reach, ensuring that the Slovak language and culture remain strong, vibrant, and accessible to future generations.
