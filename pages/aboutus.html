About Us

Who We Are
Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum – South Manchester is a registered charity dedicated to preserving and promoting Slovak language and culture. We provide education and organise cultural activities.
Our Mission
Our mission is to keep Slovak heritage alive, support families, and provide quality education for Slovak-speaking children in the UK.
Our Journey
Founded by passionate educators, our charity officially launched in 2024, creating a space for Slovak families to connect, learn, and celebrate their culture.
Our Team
Our dedicated team consists of experienced educators and charity trustees who work tirelessly to support our Slovak community abroad.
Volunteers
Our volunteers play a vital role in making a positive impact, supporting cultural events, and engaging in community activities.
How You Can Help
You can support our mission by donating, volunteering, or participating in our cultural and educational events. Together, we can keep the Slovak spirit alive!
